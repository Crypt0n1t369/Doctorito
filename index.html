Step 5 of 5
Notify your team
Choose how the coordinator communicates schedule changes and confirmations to staff. Doctors and residents can confirm or flag schedule problems directly.
📧
Email
Send weekly schedule and change notifications via email. Staff reply to confirm or flag conflicts.
💬
SMS
Text message alerts for urgent changes or last-minute shifts. High read rate for time-sensitive updates.
📱
In-app (push)
Staff view their shifts in a companion app and confirm with one tap. Full audit trail included.
📞
Automated call
Voice call for critical on-call changes. Reads shift details aloud, records verbal confirmation.
💡 How it works: When you publish or change a schedule, staff receive a notification with their shift details. They can confirm, flag a conflict, or request a swap — all tracked in one place.
